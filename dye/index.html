Rit Dye calculator

This tool is made for the liquid dye only. Calculations are based on the amounts of stuff suggested by the producer itself:

  • 3 gallons of water per 1 pound of fabric,
  • half a bottle of dye per 1 pound of fabric,
  • and 1 cup of salt per 1 gallon of water.

Normal people use metric system, so 11000 ml of water, 118 ml of dye and 750 ml of salt for 454 g of fabric.

If you try using those suggested numbers in real life though, you would quickly see that they don't make a lot of sense.
